About Me

I'm Chandler Hutchens, an Aerospace Engineer with a passion for space systems! I enjoy working on complex problems, and being a part of a team to solve them. I've worked on a variety of projects that have honed my skills in systems architecture, verification and validation, and mechanical/electrical design.

Throughout my time at Arizona State University, I worked on 4 cubesat missions, each in a varying role from technician to head systems engineer. Getting hands on experience with flight hardware

In my free time, I love watching movies, playing board games, and hiking! I enjoy traveling and meeting new people around the world, which offers new perspectives and motivations in my professional endeavors.
